LexDex Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

Overview

LexDex is a professional contact management app designed to scan, organize, and store business card information. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how LexDex handles your data.

Data Storage

All contact information scanned or entered into LexDex is stored locally on your device and optionally synced to your personal iCloud account. We do not operate any servers, do not collect your data, and do not have access to any information you store in LexDex.

iCloud Sync

If you are signed into iCloud on your device, LexDex may sync your contact data across your personal Apple devices using Apple's CloudKit service. This data is governed by Apple's privacy policy and is accessible only to you.

Camera Access

LexDex requests access to your device's camera solely for the purpose of scanning business cards. Camera images are processed on-device using Apple's Vision framework. No images are transmitted to any external server.

Data We Do Not Collect

LexDex does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or any third-party tracking services. We do not have access to your contacts, location, or any other device data beyond what is explicitly granted for camera access.

Data Security

LexDex is protected by Face ID and Touch ID authentication. Your data never leaves your device except through your personal iCloud account.

Children's Privacy

LexDex is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy please visit our support page or contact us through the App Store.
