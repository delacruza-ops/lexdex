Getting Started

Scanning a Business Card

Tap the menu icon (☰) in the top right and select "Scan Business Card." Point your camera at any business card and LexDex will automatically extract the contact information. Review the parsed fields, make any corrections, and tap Save.

Adding a Contact Manually

Tap the menu icon (☰) and select "Add Manually." Fill in the contact details, assign a tag, add any notes, and tap Save.

Tagging Contacts

Every contact can be tagged as Favorable, Neutral, or Unfavorable. Tags are color coded — green, orange, and red — for quick visual reference. Use the filter pills at the top of the contact list to filter by tag.

Exporting Contacts

Tap the menu icon (☰) and select "Export All Contacts" or "Export Current Filter" to generate a CSV file compatible with Excel and Numbers. Share it via AirDrop, email, or save to Files.

Importing Contacts

Tap the menu icon (☰) and select "Import from CSV" to import contacts from a previously exported LexDex CSV file. Duplicate contacts are automatically detected and skipped.

Face ID / Touch ID

LexDex is protected by biometric authentication. The app locks automatically when you switch away from it and requires Face ID or Touch ID to unlock.

iCloud Sync

LexDex automatically syncs your contacts across all your Apple devices via iCloud. Make sure you are signed into iCloud on all devices.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the OCR sometimes get fields wrong?

Business cards vary widely in layout and design. LexDex does its best to identify fields automatically but allows you to manually reassign any scanned line to the correct field before saving.

Is my data private?

Yes. All data is stored locally on your device and in your personal iCloud account. LexDex does not collect, transmit, or share any of your information.

Can I use LexDex without iCloud?

Yes. LexDex works fully offline. iCloud sync is optional and only activates if you are signed into iCloud on your device.

How do I back up my contacts?

Use the Export feature to save a CSV file to your iCloud Drive or any other location. This gives you a portable backup you can import back into LexDex at any time.
